Mission

As a tax-exempt, charitable nonprofit organization, we steward more than 4,100 funds created by people who care about a variety of charitable causes and interests in our region and beyond. We offer short- and long-term fund options that meet your giving and financial needs now and in the future. We also:

  • award grants through competitive grants programs to regional nonprofits;
  • award scholarships to aspiring students;
  • partner with financial advisors and estate planners to help them help their clients' charitable giving needs; and
  • convene and collaborate with others to move the needle on critical community needs and initiatives.
